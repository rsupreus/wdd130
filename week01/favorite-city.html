Favorite City

My current favorite city is Fredericksburg, TX. I lived right outside of Dallas from 2020 to 2023 and I had the chance to visit multiple cities throughout Texas. My favorite was Fredericksburg. I happen to love German food and Fredericksburg might as well be little Germany. I stayed at a local inn and got to try some of the best German food I have ever had. Schnitzel with spaetzle is definitely in my top 5 favorite foods. The way they had the mom-and-pop shops set up made it feel like I was walking down a street in Germany. I can;t wait to visit the real Germany one day so I can see how it compares.
